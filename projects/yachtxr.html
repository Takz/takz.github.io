YachtXR

Year: 2025
Platform: Apple Vision Pro
Role: XR Developer & Designer

A revolutionary yacht visualization and configuration platform that brings luxury yacht experiences to life through spatial computing. Users can explore, customize, and experience yachts in stunning photorealistic detail using Apple Vision Pro.

Project Overview

YachtXR transforms the yacht buying and charter experience by allowing clients to explore luxury vessels in unprecedented detail through spatial computing. The application leverages Apple Vision Pro's advanced capabilities to create immersive, true-to-scale yacht tours that feel remarkably real.

Users can walk through cabins, inspect materials, customize finishes, and even experience the yacht in different lighting conditions and locations - all from the comfort of a showroom or their own home.

Key Features

  • 1:1 scale spatial yacht tours
  • Real-time material and finish customization
  • Dynamic lighting and environment settings
  • Interactive hotspots with detailed information
  • Multi-user shared experiences for group viewings
  • Integrated specification sheets and documentation

Technical Challenges

The project required optimizing massive 3D yacht models for real-time rendering while maintaining photorealistic quality. We developed custom LOD systems and implemented advanced occlusion culling to ensure smooth performance.

Creating intuitive spatial UI that doesn't overwhelm users while providing deep configuration options was another key challenge we successfully addressed.

Technology Stack

SwiftUI RealityKit Reality Composer Pro USD/USDZ Metal ARKit Spatial Audio CloudKit

Results & Impact

YachtXR has revolutionized how luxury yacht dealers showcase their inventory, reducing the need for physical viewings by 60% while increasing customer engagement time by 3x. The platform has been particularly effective for international clients who can now experience yachts without traveling.

The customization features have led to a 40% increase in premium finish selections, as clients can immediately visualize their choices in context. The application has set a new standard for luxury product visualization in the marine industry.
